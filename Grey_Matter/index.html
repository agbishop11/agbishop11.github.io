Who We Are

Our Story

Xavier Rhodes founded Grey Matter in 2019 when he realized that technology was poised to fully pervade our lives. In response, he pioneered and refined a new form of design that places central focus on the psychological footprint of products and services. He hoped to simplify peoples’ lives and provide experiences that improve human wellbeing and performance.

Grey Matter is comprised of designers with expertise in human-computer interaction, applied psychology, and service design. Unlike most design consultancies that define success by usability metrics and short-term profits, we define success by long-term welfare of customers and users. All of our work supports the goals of promoting prosocial behavior and mindfulness.

Mission

Provide design consulting services for businesses and communities, leveraging the psychological sciences and benefits of mindfulness practice to better understand people and help them engage in prosocial behavior and discover wellbeing.

Vision

To elevate mindful design into an industry standard and persuade the business community to view the wellbeing of its customers and workforce as paramount to short-term valuation and shareholder returns.
